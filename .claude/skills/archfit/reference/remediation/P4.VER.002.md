# Remediation: P4.VER.002 — Untested source directories

**Summary**: Less than 70% of source directories have test files. Agents cannot verify changes in untested areas.

## Decision tree

1. **Are the untested directories auto-generated or vendored?**
   - Yes → suppress with rationale.
   - No → continue.

2. **Create test files** in the untested directories. Even a single smoke test is better than none.

3. **Re-scan** to confirm coverage improvement.

## Language-specific patterns

- Go: `*_test.go` alongside source files
- Python: `test_*.py` or `*_test.py`
- TypeScript: `*.test.ts` or `*.spec.ts`
- Java: `*Test.java` or `Test*.java`
- Ruby: `*_spec.rb` or `*_test.rb`
